# 📅 30-Day Interview Prep Plan: Intensive Sprint

> *"30 days is enough to prepare for interviews if you're already a working engineer. This plan assumes 3-4 hours/day of focused study. Prioritize ruthlessly — you can't cover everything, so cover the highest-impact topics."*

**⏱️ Estimated Time**: 10 minutes | **🎯 Difficulty**: 🟡 Medium

---

## 📋 Weekly Overview

```
WEEK 1: Foundations (coding patterns + core concepts)
WEEK 2: System Design (building blocks + practice)
WEEK 3: Advanced (hard problems + deep dives)
WEEK 4: Mock Interviews + Polish (simulation + review)

DAILY SCHEDULE (3-4 hours):
  Morning (1.5 hr):  Coding practice (2-3 problems)
  Afternoon (1 hr):  System Design study
  Evening (0.5 hr):  Behavioral prep + review
```

---

---

### 🗓️ WEEK 4: MOCK INTERVIEWS + POLISH

| Day | Focus | Details |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Day 22 | Weak Area Deep-Dive | Re-solve 3 hardest missed problems. Refine 10 STAR stories. |
| Day 23 | System Design Mock | Record yourself designing one system. Review it critically. |
| Day 24 | Coding Mock | 2 medium problems, 45 min strictly timed. |
| Day 25 | Behavioral Mock | 6 questions, full STAR answers. Use [Behavioral Guide](../Behavioral/Behavioral.md) |
| Day 26 | Weak System Design | Deep-dive your weakest design area. Use [Tradeoffs](../Tradeoffs/) |
| Day 27 | FINAL SIMULATION | Morning: coding. Afternoon: system design. Evening: behavioral. |
| Day 28 | Gap Patching | Review all feedback. Read [How to Crack System Design](./How_To_Crack_System_Design.md) |
| Day 29 | Light Review | Re-read key notes, review algorithm cheatsheet. No new material. |
| Day 30 | REST DAY | Light exercise, good sleep. Trust your preparation! |
